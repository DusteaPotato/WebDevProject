This website aims to bring together the latest information, reviews, and news about the most popular games across various genres and platforms.




GAME LIST



Age of Wonders 4

Age of Wonders 4 takes player freedom and customization in turn-based 4X games to the next level: Allowing you to design your own factions to form near limitless combinations of aesthetics, bonuses, and powerful Tomes of Magic, the game is all about choices.

Elden Ring

You could easily swap this out for any game from the Dark Souls series, but Elden Ring is more recent and it’s a real masterpiece. An open-world game unlike any other, one minute it’s oppressive and the next it’s dropping your jaw (or ripping it off).

Forza Horizon 5

Racing games are a big part of PC gaming history, but it’s typically in a slightly different gear to console, focusing on forensic simulation over arcade thrills. The Forza Horizon series proves that there’s also room for the latter.

The Witcher 3

Perhaps the best RPG ever made outside of Japan, The Witcher 3 is epic and all-encompassing. In it, you take control of a witcher. What you don’t expect, based on that premise, are side stories about spousal abuse, class divides, and what loneliness does to a person.
