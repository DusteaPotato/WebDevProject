Top 10 Mobile Games


GAME LIST



Fortnite

Fortnite is an online video game developed by Epic Games and released in 2017. It is available in three distinct game mode versions that otherwise share the same general gameplay and game engine.

Genshin Impact

Travel across the seven nations and meet unique, powerful allies. Get more rare characters and weapons in the new version. Highlights: Creating Immersive Virtual World Experiences, Using Cutting-Edge Technologies.

Roblox

Roblox is an online game platform and game creation system developed by Roblox Corporation that allows users to program games and play games created by other users.Roblox is a global platform that brings people together through play.

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang

Mobile Legends: Bang Bang is a mobile multiplayer online battle arena game developed and published by Moonton, a subsidiary of ByteDance. Released in 2016, the game grew in popularity; most prominently in Southeast Asia.

Call of Duty: Mobile

CODM is a shooter game that is two games in one. One pits players against each other in classic team-based game modes; the other is a battle royale like PUBG. Along with the fast-paced gameplay, CoD Mobile offers players impressive realistic visuals without a drop in performance.

Player Unknown's BattleGround: Mobile

PUBG Mobile is a free-to-play battle royale video game developed by LightSpeed & Quantum Studio, a division of Tencent Games. It was initially released for Android and iOS on 19 March 2018.

League of Legends: Wild Rift

League of Legends: Wild Rift is a multiplayer online battle arena mobile game developed and published by Riot Games for Android and iOS. The free-to-play game is a modified version of the PC game League of Legends.

Pokémon GO

Pokémon Go is a 2016 augmented reality mobile game, part of the Pokémon franchise, developed and published by Niantic in collaboration with Nintendo and The Pokémon Company for iOS and Android devices.

Apex Legends: Mobile

Apex Legends: Mobile is a smooth-as-butter battle royale from the team that brought you Titanfall 2. On top of Respawn Entertainment's shooter chops, also designed to modernize the genre with reduced downtime between matches. Adaptive mobile version of Apex Legend

Clash of Clans

Clash of Clans is a 2012 free-to-play mobile strategy video game developed and published by Finnish game developer Supercell. The game was released for iOS platforms on August 2, 2012, and on Google Play for Android on October 7, 2013.
